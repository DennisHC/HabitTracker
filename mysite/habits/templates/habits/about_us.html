Our Story


Evergreen Habit Tracker was developed by two Senior Computer Science students studying at the University of California, Irvine.
With a passion in bringing together technology and other disciplines such as productivity and pscyhology, the Evergreen Habit Tracker was created.

Why a Habit Tracker?

This habit tracker was chosen as a project to combine software development with productivity, psychology, and holistic well-being.
By combining these disciplines together, the by product of this app is to reinforce positive habits and attaining goals.
